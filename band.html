Band Members

  • Freddie Mercury (Lead vocals, piano)
  • Brian May (Lead guitar, vocals)
  • Roger Taylor (Drums, vocals)
  • John Deacon (Bass guitar)

Top 3 Albums

A Night at the Opera (1975)
This album is a masterpiece of rock, featuring the epic "Bohemian Rhapsody." It blends hard rock, opera, and pop seamlessly.
Sheer Heart Attack (1974)
A high-energy album that cemented their signature sound. It features "Killer Queen" and is often considered the band's most balanced hard-rock effort.
News of the World (1977)
Featuring the arena anthems "We Will Rock You" and "We Are the Champions," this album showcases a more raw and driving sound.
