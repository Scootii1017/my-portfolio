fries

A delicious side dish that uses potatos.

Ingredients

  • Potatoes: 3 large (Russet or starchy potatoes work best)
  • Cornflour/Cornstarch: 1–2 tablespoons (optional, for extra crispiness)
  • Oil: For deep frying

Instructions

  1. Preparation and Soaking
  2. Cut: Peel the potatoes (optional) and cut them into 1 cm thick sticks.
  3. Soak: Submerge the potato sticks in a bowl of cold water (or ice-cold water) for 30–45 minutes to remove excess starch, which helps make them crispy.
  4. Dry: Drain the water and rinse the potatoes again. Crucially, pat them completely dry using a kitchen towel or paper napkin.
  5. Heat Oil: Heat oil in a wok (kadai) over medium-low heat (approx. 135°C/275°F).
  6. Fry: Add potatoes in batches, ensuring you do not overcrowd the pan.
  7. Cook: Fry for 3-4 minutes. They should be cooked through but not brown.
